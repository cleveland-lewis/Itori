## Potential Apple Review Questions

### Q: "You mention AI features. What data is sent?"
**A**: Users provide their own API keys. Data goes directly from user's device to their chosen AI service (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.). We don't process, store, or see any of this data. This is clearly disclosed in Section 3.

### Q: "You use iCloud. Explain data access."
**A**: iCloud sync is optional and uses the user's personal iCloud account. We do not have access to user iCloud data. Apple handles all storage and transmission. Disclosed in Section 2.

### Q: "What about calendar data?"
**A**: Calendar access is optional, read-only, processed locally, never transmitted. Clearly explained in Section 4.

### Q: "How do you handle children's data?"
**A**: We don't collect any data from anyone, including children. The app is safe for all ages and complies with COPPA. Stated in Section 5.
