# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 8, 2026**

## Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Itori, we believe your academic data is yours and yours alone. This privacy policy is simple: **we don't collect, transmit, or share any of your personal data**.

---

## 1. Data Collection

**We do not collect any data.**

- No personal information
- No usage analytics
- No tracking or telemetry
- No advertising data
- No device identifiers

All information you enter into Itori stays on your device.

---

## 2. Data Storage

### Local Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using Apple's Core Data framework:

- Assignments and tasks
- Course information
- Grades and academic records
- Study timer sessions
- Flashcard decks
- App settings and preferences

### iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you choose to enable iCloud sync:

- Your data syncs through **your personal iCloud account**
- We do not have access to your iCloud data
- Apple handles all data transmission and storage
- You can disable iCloud sync anytime in Settings
- Subject to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

Itori does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, advertising, or data collection services.

### Optional AI Features

If you choose to use AI-powered features with your own API keys:

- You control which AI service to use (OpenAI, Anthropic, local models, etc.)
- Data is sent directly to your chosen service, not through our servers
- We do not store, access, or monitor your API keys or AI interactions
- Subject to your chosen service's privacy policy
- AI features are completely optional and can be disabled

---

## 4. Permissions

### Calendar Access (Optional)

If you grant calendar permission:

- The app reads your calendar events locally
- No calendar data is transmitted externally
- Used only for displaying school events and syncing assignments

### Notifications (Optional)

If you enable notifications:

- All notifications are generated locally on your device
- No notification data is collected or transmitted

---

## 5. Data Security

- All data is stored using Apple's secure Core Data framework
- iCloud sync uses end-to-end encryption
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No user accounts or passwords to manage

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

Itori is safe for users of all ages. Since we don't collect any data, we comply with all children's privacy regulations including COPPA and GDPR.

---

## 7. Your Rights

Since we don't collect your data, you maintain complete control:

- **Access:** All your data is accessible in the app
- **Export:** You can export your data anytime
- **Delete:** Delete the app to remove all local data
- **Control:** Manage iCloud sync in Settings

---

## 8. International Users

Itori works the same way worldwide - with zero data collection. Your data never leaves your device except through optional iCloud sync (using your own iCloud account).

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy:

- We will update the "Last Updated" date
- Changes will be communicated through app updates
- You can always view the latest policy at [itori.app/privacy](https://itori.app/privacy.html)

---

We'll respond within 48 hours.

## Summary

**The simple version:** We built Itori to respect your privacy completely. We don't collect, store, or share any of your data. Everything stays on your device (and optionally in your personal iCloud account). No tracking, no ads, no data mining. Your academic information is yours alone.

This privacy policy complies with:

- ✅ **Apple App Store Requirements**
